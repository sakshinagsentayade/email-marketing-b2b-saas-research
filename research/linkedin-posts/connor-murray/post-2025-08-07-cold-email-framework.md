## Full Post Text:
Connor Murray built an outbound system that books 3 to 5 meetings a week.

The framework he uses is simple:
1. Who you are
2. Why you’re reaching out
3. What you want

He also says the key is frequency and clarity.

Here’s the follow-up cadence he recommends:
- Email 1: Monday
- Follow-up 1: Wednesday
- Follow-up 2: Friday
- Follow-up 3: Next Monday

His main advice is to avoid bloated emails, use assumptive language, and keep your outreach short and clear.
---

## Key Takeaways:- Use a simple 3-part email structure.- Follow up every 24 to 48 hours.- Keep messages short and clear.- Track open, reply, and meeting rates.

## How I'll use this:I will keep cold email simple, use a repeatable structure, and follow up on a consistent schedule.
