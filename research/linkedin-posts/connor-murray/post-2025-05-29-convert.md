## Full Post Text:
Connor Murray says the best cold emails in 2025 follow a basic flow:

1. Observation
2. Relevance
3. Value
4. Call to Action

He gives a simple example:
- Show that you did your research.
- Explain why you are reaching out now.
- Share the value you can offer.
- End with a light ask.

He also says the best cold emails are short, direct, and easy to scan.

Example:"Hey Sarah, saw your post about launching a new marketplace vertical at [Company] last week. I'm a former marketplace CTO now building AI agents that detect fraud and automate customer conversations. Curious if that's something you're exploring. Worth a quick chat?"

---
## Key Takeaways:
- Use a clear four-part structure.
- Start with observation and relevance.
- Keep the email short.
- End with a simple ask.

## How I'll use this:
I will write cold emails that show research, explain value quickly, and make a clear but low-pressure ask.
