## Full Post Text:
Connor Murray explains a simple cold email framework for B2B sales.

The main structure he recommends is:
1. Who you are
2. Why you are reaching out
3. What you want

He also says that cold emails should be short, relevant, and easy to read.

He recommends tracking:
- Open rate- 
Reply rate
- Meeting rate

He also says that follow-ups should be sent every 24 to 48 hours.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Keep cold emails short.
- Use a simple message structure.
- Track open, reply, and meeting rates.
- Follow up within 24 to 48 hours.

## How I'll use this:
I will keep outreach emails short and clear, and I will measure whether people open, reply, and book meetings.
