## Full Post Text:

Connor Murray says the A/B/A/B cadence helps keep cold email organized and effective.

The idea is to split prospects into two groups and alternate outreach so you can stay consistent without spamming the same people too fast.

He recommends:
- Short emails.
- Follow-ups every 24 to 48 hours.
- Clear, direct language.
- Tracking replies and meetings.

He also says emailing earlier in the day often works better because it feels more thoughtful and timely.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Use a simple follow-up cadence.
- Split prospects into groups.
- Keep emails short and direct.
- Send messages early in the day.
