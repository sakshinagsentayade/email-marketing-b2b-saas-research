## Full Post Text:

A zero-click web does not mean the death of marketing.

It means marketing has to evolve.

Rand Fishkin says we are no longer just optimizing for traffic.

We are optimizing for visibility, credibility, and influence at the moment of discovery. 

That changes how marketers should think about content and distribution.

Instead of focusing only on clicks, you need to create value in the feed, earn attention there, and then move people into a permissioned channel you own. 

The big idea is simple: if the platforms are holding the audience, your marketing has to work there first.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Zero-click behavior is changing marketing.
- Visibility matters as much as traffic.
- Discovery now happens on platforms.
- Earn attention where people already are.

## How I'll use this:
I will create content that builds trust and attention on platforms before trying to move people elsewhere.
