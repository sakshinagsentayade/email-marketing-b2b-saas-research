## Full Post Text:

The most important question David Cancel says every founder should ask is: why now?

It is easy to fall in love with an idea.

But an idea alone is not enough. Timing matters just as much as the concept itself. 

If the world is not ready, or the problem is not urgent, the company may struggle no matter how good the execution is.

David’s point is that founders should pressure-test the moment, not just the idea.

Why now? Why this problem? Why is this the right time to build?

That question forces clarity and helps separate real opportunity from wishful thinking.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Timing is a core part of company strategy.
- A strong idea still needs the right moment.
- Founders should pressure-test “why now?”
- Clarity about timing improves decision-making.

## How I'll use this:
I will ask whether the market is ready right now, not just whether the idea sounds exciting.
