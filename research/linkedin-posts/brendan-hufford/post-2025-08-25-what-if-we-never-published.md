## Full Post Text:

“What would happen if we never published this?”

Brendan Hufford says this is one of the best questions you can ask before making a piece of content. If the answer is “nothing,” you may be creating checkbox content. 

The point is not to publish more just to stay busy.

The point is to create content that:
- Earns trust.
- Accelerates decisions.
- Gets reused by sales and marketing.
- Helps buyers move forward.

Brendan’s larger idea is that content should match buyer intent and stage, not just fill a calendar.

That means awareness content should build trust, mid-stage content should bring clarity, and late-stage content should remove friction. 

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Ask what breaks if the content does not exist.
- Content should serve a clear job.
- Match content to buyer intent and stage.
- Useful content gets reused, shared, and sold with.

## How I'll use this:
I will only create content that would matter if it disappeared, and I will make sure it serves a clear buyer stage.
