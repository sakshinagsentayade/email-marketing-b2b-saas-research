## Full Post Text:

Here’s the world’s easiest and most effective content strategy for 2025:

Pick one problem your customers have, then write around it in five different ways.

Brendan Hufford’s framework is:
- How to solve your customer’s problem.
- How to solve the first roadblock in that problem.
- Useful templates that help solve the problem.
- Customer case studies that show the problem being solved.
- A high-level roundup of people solving that problem.

The point is not to create random content.

The point is to build a set of posts around one clear customer problem, then move on to the next one.

That gives you months of content that can actually drive revenue.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Choose one customer problem.
- Create five content pieces around it.
- Repeat until the problem is fully covered.
- Focus on revenue, not random posting.

## How I'll use this:
I will build my content in clusters around one customer problem instead of trying to cover too many topics at once.
