## Full Post Text:

When I asked what most founders get wrong about outbound, he said, “Most of their tactics are from 2019.”

Here’s what works in 2025:

1. Subject lines: keep it to 3 words max.
2. Clear value: each email should offer exactly one clear benefit.
3. Social triggers: reference recent funding, LinkedIn activities, or mutual connections.
4. Problem-focused: directly address specific pain points your prospect is facing.
5. Follow-ups win: new angles every 3 days close deals faster than pressure.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Cold email needs to be simple.
- Short subject lines work better.
- Every email should be focused on one clear value.
- Follow-ups matter.

## How I'll use this:
I will keep my outreach short, clear, and problem-focused when writing cold emails.
