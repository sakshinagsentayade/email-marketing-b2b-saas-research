## Full Post Text:

In 2025, everything seems so much harder.

Most SaaS founders are going to fail this year because they're running playbooks that worked in 2020.

Here are the 7 things I see that ARE working - in a world where most things are NOT:

1. Creating a truly AI-first startup
2. Word-of-mouth
3. Personal brand
4. Cold email, in the right context
5. Thought leadership ads
6. Cold calls
7. Staying ridiculously lean

The main idea is that old growth strategies are not enough anymore. Founders need better positioning, stronger content, and leaner execution.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Old SaaS playbooks may not work in 2025.
- Personal brand is a strong growth lever.
- Cold email still works in some situations.
- Staying lean helps founders move faster.

## How I'll use this:
I will remember that modern SaaS growth needs stronger content, better targeting, and efficient execution.
