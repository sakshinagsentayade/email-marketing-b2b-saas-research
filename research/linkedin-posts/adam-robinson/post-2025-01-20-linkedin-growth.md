## Full Post Text:
In 2024, I got 21M views, added 61k followers and drove $4M ARR from LinkedIn.

But I spent 65% of my time creating content, going on podcasts, hosting events.

Here’s how I’m optimizing my LinkedIn playbook for 2025:

2024 Playbook:
- 1 work-in-public LinkedIn post
- 2 “thought leadership” LinkedIn posts
- 1 narrative LinkedIn post
- 3-5 podcast guest slots- 1 Inbound-Led Outbound Workshop
- 1 keynote per month at IRL events
- 1 new season of Billion Dollar Challenge
- 1 newsletter
- All guest slot podcasts and short-form videos posted to Spotify and Apple
- Responding to nearly every LinkedIn comment daily

2025 Playbook:
- 5 LinkedIn posts per week, planned in advance
- Diana posts 1x/week, then grows to 3x/week- Weekly live workshop
- Grow founder community
- More events, newsletters, podcasts, and workshops
- Respond to every comment again

LinkedIn has changed my life.
