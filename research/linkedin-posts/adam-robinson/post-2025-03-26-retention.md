## Full Post Text:

Adam Robinson shares ideas about how retention and re-engagement can grow a business.

The main idea is that keeping customers is just as important as getting new customers.

If people stop using your product, you should understand why they left and try to bring them back.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Retention matters as much as acquisition.
- Understand why customers leave.
- Re-engagement can help growth.
- Email is useful for bringing users back.

## How I'll use this:
I will remember that email marketing is not only for getting new leads, but also for keeping existing customers active.
