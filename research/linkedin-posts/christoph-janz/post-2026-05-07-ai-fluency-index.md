## Full Post Text:

Christoph Janz announced the launch of the P9 AI Fluency Index.

He said one of the big challenges for many founders right now is getting their organizations fully “AI-pilled.” 

The goal of the index is to help founders and CEOs benchmark their company’s AI fluency and get recommendations on how to move to the next level. 

He explained that the index is grounded in published rubrics and posts from companies like Zapier, Finai, Ramp, Jobber, Shopify, and Anthropic. 

The post shows that Janz is thinking not just about AI as a market, but about how companies actually adopt and operationalize it.

## Key Takeaways:
