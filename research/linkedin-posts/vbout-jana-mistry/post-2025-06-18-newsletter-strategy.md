## Full Post Text:

A B2B email newsletter should do more than fill inboxes.

It should build trust, guide readers through the customer journey, and support real business goals.

In this VBOUT session, Jana Mistry breaks down the basics of building a newsletter strategy that works:
- Define your goals before you send anything.
- Understand your audience and what content resonates with them.
- Avoid random content and purchased lists.
- Match content to the funnel stage, from top to bottom.
- Use personalization, segmentation, and enrichment data carefully.

She also explains that consistency matters, but so does relevance.

The best newsletters are not just frequent.

They are useful, intentional, and built for the people reading them.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Start with strategy, not just sending.
- Match newsletter content to the customer journey.
- Personalization and segmentation improve relevance.
- Consistency only works when the content is useful.

## How I'll use this:
I will treat the newsletter as a customer journey tool, not just a broadcast channel.
