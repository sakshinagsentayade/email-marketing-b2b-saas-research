## What He Said (Full Text):

In 2018, people told me: "You already failed 3 businesses, get a real job"

In 2019, people told me: "lemlist will fail, the market is too competitive"

In 2020, people told me: "Your growth isn't real, you don't have product market fit"

In 2021, people told me: "Your $150M valuation is just because of market trends"

In 2024, people told me: "Your market isn't big enough to grow more"

In 2025, people said: "$45M ARR and $15M EBITDA is amazing! Are you still hiring?"

My lesson: If people doubt you, go so far they can't hear you anymore.

---

## What I Learned (Key Points):

1. Don't listen to people who doubt you
2. lemlist grew to $45M per year even with critics
3. You can succeed without investors (bootstrapping)
4. Focus on making revenue, not just getting famous
