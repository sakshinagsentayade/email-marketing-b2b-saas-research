## Full Post Text:
If you’re running B2B outbound or nurturing high-intent leads, your inbox reputation matters a lot.

Evan Kozliner says the biggest deliverability lever is getting a real reply right after a prospect books a call.

Why this matters:
1. Mail providers care about engagement.
2. A reply sends a positive signal that your emails are wanted.
3. The moment after a call is booked is the best time to lock in good engagement.

The idea is to make your first post-booking email easy to reply to.

A simple “Yes” or “Y” can help improve sender reputation and inbox placement.

---
## Key Takeaways:
- Deliverability depends on engagement.
- A quick reply can improve sender reputation.
- The post-booking moment is important.
- Small engagement gains can help the whole sequence.

## How I'll use this:
I will make post-booking emails short and easy to reply to so they help deliverability and keep prospects engaged.
