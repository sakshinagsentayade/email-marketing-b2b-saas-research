## Full Post Text:
Every solopreneur should have a newsletter.

Evan Kozliner says newsletters are the beating heart of a business and the direct line to your audience.

His main points are:
- A newsletter helps you own your audience.
- It creates a direct, permission-based relationship.
- It can support trust, retention, upsells, and repeat revenue.
- It is especially useful for solo operators who need a scalable communication channel.

He also says newsletters can become the glue that keeps people connected to your brand and work.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Newsletters help you own your audience.
- They support trust and repeat revenue.
- They are important for solo businesses.
- They are one of the best direct channels you can control.

## How I'll use this:
I will treat newsletters as an important way to build audience ownership and keep people connected to the business.
