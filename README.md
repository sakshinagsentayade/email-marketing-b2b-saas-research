# Email Marketing for B2B SaaS Research

This repository collects real-world  content from 10 practitioners who actively work on email marketing, cold outreach, newsletter growth, and B2B SaaS communication.

# Motivation
The goal of this project is to study how strong B2B SaaS operators actually use email and related channels in practice, not just in theory.

# Why these experts
I chose founders, operators,creators, and marketers who have built real SaaS companies, grown audiences, or taught email strategy from hands-on experience.

# Method
I collected recent content from each expert using a mix of:
-LinkedIn posts.
-YouTube transcripts.
-Podcast episodes.
-Founder spotlights.
-Oher social posts

I organized each source into Markdown so the materail is easy to read, compare,and reuse later.

## Repository overview
- 'research/sources/' -experts.md -Master list of all experts with links,dates, and short annotations.
- 'research/linkedin-posts/' -recent LinkedIn posts organized by author.
- 'research/youtube-transcripts/' -Youtube transcripts organized by video.
- 'research/others/' -podcast episodes, founder spotlights, and social posts.
- 'README.md' -overview of the project and collection method.

  Files are named with the author name, topic plus the date so they are easy to search and reference later.

  -'research/others'
  --- 'connor-murray'
  ----- 'podcast-2025-06-08.md'

  ## How to extend this research
  1. Add a new expert to 'research/sources'.
  2. Save their content in the correct folder.
  3. Keep filenames consistent.
  4. Commit changes regularly as new material is added.
